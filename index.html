My name is Jasmine.

Born in Hong Kong, went to high school in New Zealand, and took a gap year in Australia, I am a currently rising junior at the University of Southern California (USC), studying Computer Science.


My journey as a developer began during lockdown when I took Tap Tap Kaboom's class and built a super simple Minions wikipedia. Fast forward to today, I have had the privilege of interning as a Software Engineer at companies like LinkedIn, Serato, USC Spatial Sciences Institute and Sara.


While studying abroad in Sydney, I found my interest in entrepreneurship and product development. Through participating in (and winning) multiple hackathons and start-up incubators, I was able to tackle real-world challenges and ship products under tight deadlines.


I am passionate about building products at the intersection of technology, creativity, and social impact. Outside of coding, I enjoy rock climbing, working out, going on spontaneous trips, watching sit-coms.
